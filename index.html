I Hate RLCraft.

Here's Why!

RLCraft is a modpack for Minecraft focused on making the game of Minecraft (a game of inifinite possibility) as realistic as possible. Therefore:

  • You need water.
  • You get cold and hot.
  • You have to increase your skill in something before you can use certain items.
  • You can't just punch trees.
  • Most importantly, you die a lot.
